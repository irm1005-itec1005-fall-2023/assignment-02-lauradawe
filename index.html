About Us

Sprinkles strives to reach top level quality and aesthetic for all of our customers. We're completely nut, sesame, egg, honey and gelatine free. We offer gluten free options, vegan options, soy free options and sunflower free options etc. without compromising on the soft, creamy richness of a great cake. Curds, batters, pastries and even ice creams are made in house using ingredients which are organic and Canadian whenever possible. Never too sweet and always the very best flavour, our treats are sure to please your guests or that mid afternoon craving.

Cake Designs

We make cakes simple to check off your to do list with designs that all your guests will love. They act as the perfect canvas for any occasion. Each of our tiered cakes have multiple flavours to make sure all your guests will get what they enjoy.

Delivery

To keep your cake safe, it's best for us to deliver, however you can absolutely pick them up (2 people are best). Delivery starts at $150. We also offer set up with the delivery starting at $200.

Rentals

We offer pedestals, towers, and platters of all sizes, colours and shapes. You can rent them if you are getting treats elsewhere too, we don't mind! Cupcake towers rent for $50, while pedestals rent out for $15/ea. Rentals are due back within 3 days.

Consultations

We do not offer in house consultations. We create a sample box for you to try our flavours, the website is loaded with information for you to take a look at, then we ring you to chat about any questions and put your order together.

Cupcake Collection

We offer all-natural, gourmet cupcakes baked fresh daily, made from the finest ingredients, and piped to perfection with no artificial flavours, lard, or preservatives.
